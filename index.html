01 / About

More than another pickup game.

h030ps creates consistent basketball runs where people can compete, improvise, connect and lose track of time.

We approach basketball like an ensemble: every player brings their own style, but the best moments happen when everyone understands the rhythm of the group.

The goal is to build a welcoming but competitive basketball community that extends beyond the court through music, media, events and collaborations.
